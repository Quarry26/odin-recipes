Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta.
Made of several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • 1 x Onion
  • 2 x Cloves of garlic
  • 1 x Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 x Cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 x Cans of tomato paste
  • 2 x tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozerella & ricotta cheese
  • 1 x Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
